# Photography

For many years, photography has been my way of capturing the atmosphere of a moment, freezing time outside of the terminal window.

I post regular snapshots on [:fab fa-instagram: Instagram](https://instagram.com/vojay.io), but this page serves as a curated selection of my personal favorites.

> **Collaboration:** I believe the best way to master a craft is through experimentation. If you have a creative idea or an interesting project, I am open to collaborating **for free** to learn and grow, schedule permitting.
